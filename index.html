[ about ]

Hello — I'm a behavioral scientist working at the intersection of communication neuroscience, political psychology, and social neuroscience.

My research explores how people process and respond to persuasive messages, how neural and psychological mechanisms shape political attitudes, and how social context influences brain function and behavior.

I use neuroimaging (fMRI), behavioral experiments, and computational methods to understand the cognitive and neural underpinnings of communication and social influence.
